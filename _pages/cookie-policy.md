## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website.
They help websites remember settings, improve performance, and support analytics/advertising.

## How This Site Uses Cookies

This site may use cookies for:

- traffic analytics and performance insights
- ad delivery and ad measurement
- basic site behavior and preference handling

## Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services (such as analytics and advertising providers) may set their own cookies according to their policies.

## Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.
Disabling some cookies may affect site functionality or ad relevance.
